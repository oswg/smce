One more SCF session from _Star Wards_ before we return to your regular programming. This is a little bit silly, but this Korton fella strikes me a little bit as a call screener for the Confederation; he typically introduces and plays off contacts, but doesn't share much himself. So this is a rare question-and-answer time with him where he addresses several concern of the Solar Cross group. 

And one more random and idle thought: because Korton functions so often in this material as a kind of pass-through entity, starting and ending communications but handing off to another complex like Monka or Voltra or Soltec, part of me wonders whether there isn't some connection between Korton and Q'uo somehow. Q'uo also functions as a proxy for several complexes, although more as a synthesizer than as a talk show host. I fully acknowledge this is stupid and insane, but when reading this material for thru lines it is helpful to keep your mind _just open enough_ that only a small amount of brain falls out.

<!-- more -->

In this answer to a question, Korton takes the concept of the "Light" that those of Hatonn discussed last time and applies it to how seekers might band together to better accomplish the mission to which the Confederation is clueing them in. Calling it "the Light" seems overblown when we're just talking about the energy dynamics within and between individuals, but I do think a big part of functioning groups is a minimum recognition of the energy flowing between people, recognizing when it's time to (as we used to say in Occupy) step forward and then step back. 

> **Question**: How can we meld the individuals in the group into a functioning whole? Please help us define and clarify our priorities. I personally am "flying blind" and tend to try to cover the waterfront. It would be most helpful, for example, if I knew what I should zero in on. Thank you, 
> 
> **Korton**: Your inquiry is one that is most apropos since it is already ‘a question listed to be asked. I will answer your question in three separate parts: The unity of those in your midst, who receive these communications and who also participate in various avenues of service, can best be accomplished by your use of that which we have described s the "Light" of The Radiant One. 
> 
> This statement also answers the second part of your question as to what is of first priority that all of you may participate in. My brothers and sisters, until the Tensor Concept material is presented for your thought and consideration, it is our suggestion that you study carefully the transcripts and your recordings of our discussions on the "Light." Each of you have the opportunity to invoke this "Light" and apply it to a wide range of problems you encounter or that are brought before your consideration. Also, that you invoke its use so that each of you, in turn, can becone familiar with its feeling and use. When one becomes attuned to the conscious employment of the ‘Light" and senses its direct usefulness in all your activities, then, at that time you will be ready to consider, in the "Light," the Tensor Concept materials and will be able to evaluate them properly for presentation to the peoples on the planet earth. 
> 
> Therefore, create the essence of unity by the use of the "Light." It is our suggestion that each of you practice diligently those ways of using the "Light"; using it for problem-solving, using it in your every-day activities, using it to protect and to stimulate your own awareness. The "Light" may be applied to all situations. This will allow each individual, regardless of the degree of awareness attained, £0 enhance their ability to a remarkable degree. 
> 
> I believe that in this answer to your inquiry we have also answered the third part of your question, so that now you need but apply your efforts, interest and concentration along those avenues just suggested to realize a quickly as possible that which it is you desire to know. 

\- [Korton via Miller: September 1, 1974](https://github.com/oswg/akasha/blob/main/scf/misc/1974-09-01_scf_misc_004.md)
